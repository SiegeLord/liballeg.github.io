Logos

Page 1 - 2 - 3

The following four logos were created and donated by , a software developer who joined the Allegro community not very long ago, and wanted to give back something in exchange for the excellent library. The logos were made with Gimp and you are free to use and distribute them. In fact, if you want them with other dimensions, colors or texts, just email him personally, since he has the sources and can redraw them according to your suggestions. Each logo is sized 500x256 and weighs around 60KB. Click on the thumbnails to view the full version.

A long time ago, in a mailing list far, far away... Peter Wang said: 'Given that we use "AL" so much, why have we not adopted a fat, drunk couch potato for a mascot?'. Later, Grzegorz Godlewski, being unable to forget this sentence, asked Karol Krenski, who makes GPL pictures for different open source projects, to draw the very same picture you can see to the right of this text. Impressed? Hold your breath, because you can go to Karol's web page to see this logo and many more, like the ones he drew for SDL or Postfix. He also has the sources, so you could rescale this image or ask him to do any modifications you may want.

Bruno S. Drago made the small logo to the left for one of his games and contributed it to the Allegro community. Bruno keeps working on his games, which you can find at: http://www.xuti.net/.
